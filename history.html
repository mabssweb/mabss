Discover Our Story

School Background

Mbarara Army Boarding Secondary School was established in 2004 with the vision of providing quality education infused with military discipline and patriotic values. Founded by the Uganda People's Defence Forces (UPDF), the school aimed to create an institution that would nurture future leaders with strong moral character and academic excellence.

Located on the scenic hills of Makenke,Mbarara, Western Uganda, Along Mbarara-Masaka highway road. The school began with just 120 students and 15 staff members. Over the years, it has grown significantly, now accommodating over 800 students from various regions of Uganda.

The school's unique approach combines rigorous academic training with military-style discipline, producing graduates who excel not only in academics but also in character development and leadership skills.

Today, Mbarara Army Boarding Secondary School stands as one of the premier educational institutions in the region, consistently ranking among the top schools in national examinations and producing citizens who contribute meaningfully to Uganda's development.
