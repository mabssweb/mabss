PATRIOTISM PROGRAM

At MABSS, we instill strong patriotic values in our students through various activities:

  • Daily flag raising ceremonies with School, National and East African Anthems
  • History lessons focusing on Uganda's heritage
  • Participation in national celebrations
  • Community service projects
  • Leadership training with emphasis on national service

Our patriotism program helps students develop love for their country and prepares them to be responsible citizens.

SCOUTS & GUIDES

Our Scouts and Guides program focuses on character development and outdoor skills:

  • Weekly meetings and training sessions
  • Camping and hiking expeditions
  • First aid and survival skills training
  • Community service projects
  • Participation in national jamborees

The program helps students develop leadership, teamwork, and practical life skills while fostering a spirit of service.
