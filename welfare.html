Services offered

Ensuring the well-being and holistic development of every student

Our Welfare Services

1 Boarding Facilities

We provide comfortable and secure boarding facilities with:

  • Spacious dormitories with bunk beds
  • 24/7 security and supervision beefed up by security cameras
  • Regular dormitory inspections for cleanliness

2 Health Services

Our health services include:

  • Fully equipped school clinic
  • Three qualified nurses on duty 24/7
  • Partnership with 2DIV Military Hospital
  • Regular health check-ups & Pregnancy tests
  • Health education programs, Regular Seniorman & Seniorwoman talks
  • First aid training for students & staff

3 Career Guidance & Counseling Services

Our Career guidance & Counseling department offers:

  • Professional school counselors
  • Confidential counseling sessions
  • Career guidance for individual classess
  • Peer counseling programs
  • Stress management services
  • Drug abuse prevention programs,etc.

Welfare Policies

Anti-Bullying Policy

We maintain a zero-tolerance policy towards bullying in any form. Our anti-bullying measures include:

  • Clear reporting procedures
  • Education programs on bullying prevention
  • Support systems for victims
  • Restorative justice approaches

Child Protection Policy

We are committed to safeguarding all students with:

  • Rigorous staff vetting procedures
  • Mandatory child protection training for all staff
  • Clear guidelines for staff-student interactions
  • Confidential reporting mechanisms

Discipline Policy

Our discipline system focuses on positive behavior reinforcement with:

  • Clear code of conduct
  • Progressive discipline approach
  • Opportunities for behavior improvement
  • Parental involvement in serious cases
