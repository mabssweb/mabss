Academic Programs

Ordinary Level (S1-S4)

Our 'O' Level program offers a comprehensive and rich curriculum with sciences, humanities, and vocational subjects to prepare students for both academic and practical life. Kiswahili is compulsory from S1-S4.

Advanced Level (S5-S6)

The 'A' Level program offers specialization in Sciences, Arts, and Humanities with excellent teaching staff and modern facilities to ensure student success.

Capacity Building Workshop 2025

Empowering Educators for Excellence: Staff Development Initiative

The Capacity Building Workshop 2025 at Mbarara Army Boarding Secondary School (MABSS) was held on 24th October 2025 under the theme “Promoting Academic Excellence for Holistic Growth.” The event featured sessions on financial literacy, retirement preparation, ICT integration in education, and building resilience in family and workplace life. It brought together teachers and staff to share knowledge, develop new skills, and strengthen teamwork. Participants learned the value of financial planning, effective use of technology in teaching, and maintaining a healthy work–life balance.
