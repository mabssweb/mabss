Admission Services

Application Process

  1. Collect application form from school office or download online
  2. Complete all required sections
  3. Attach required documents (reports, birth certificate, etc.)
  4. Submit with application fee
  5. Attend interview if required
  6. Receive admission letter
Apply online

Requirements

O-Level (S1-S4)

  • Completed PLE results slip
  • Birth certificate
  • Passport photos (4 copies)
  • Transfer letter (if transferring)
  • Medical form (provided by school)

A-Level (S5-S6)

  • Completed UCE results slip
  • Birth certificate
  • Passport photos (4 copies)
  • Transfer letter (if transferring)
  • Medical form (provided by school)
